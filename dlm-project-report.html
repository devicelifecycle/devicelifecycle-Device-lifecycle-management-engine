Project Overview

Why this was built, what it solves, and where everything stands.

📬

Replace Email + Phone Chaos

Sales enters and tracks every CPO + Trade request in one portal. No more lost emails, no more "where am I at with this one?"

🌐

Customer Self-Service Portal

Corporate customers request CPO + Trade directly without going through a sales rep. Sales becomes optional, not the bottleneck.

🏭

Center of Excellence

Triage, QC, repair, refurb — all in one system. Multi-vendor splits (300/200/500) feel like one seamless order to the customer.

The Real Pain Points Being Solved

No date stamping. Requests arrived and disappeared. No one knew what was fulfilled and what was forgotten.

Manual email management. Humans wrote follow-up emails. Now automated and tied to time — "it's been 48 hours, not fulfilled."

Single vendor bottleneck. Growth was capped by one vendor's capacity. Now any number of vendors can bid and fulfill in parallel.

Pricing was guesswork. No competitor data. Now 5 competitors scraped daily, pricing calculated algorithmically from market data.
